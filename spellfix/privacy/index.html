Privacy

A privacy policy with nothing to hide.

SpellFix runs entirely on your computer. We don't collect anything. The short version: there's nothing to opt out of, because there's nothing being collected.

Last updated: April 2026 · Applies to SpellFix for macOS

Information we collect

None. SpellFix has no account system, no telemetry, no analytics, no crash reporter. Your text, your settings, your downloaded models and your purchase status all live on your Mac.

On-device processing

Correction, reshaping and translation run locally using AI models stored on your computer — Apple Foundation Model by default, or any other local model you choose to download (Qwen, Gemma, SmolLM, and others). SpellFix makes no network request during these operations. You can verify this with Little Snitch or any equivalent network monitor.

The only network activity SpellFix may perform is:

  • Checking for updates through the Mac App Store (handled by macOS itself).
  • Downloading additional AI models, only when you explicitly request a new model from Settings → Models.
  • Validating your App Store purchase via StoreKit (handled by macOS).

These connections go to Apple's servers and are governed by Apple's Privacy Policy. SpellFix does not have its own backend.

In-app purchase

The Pro upgrade is sold through Apple's App Store. We do not receive your payment information, name, or address. Apple provides us only with anonymized aggregate purchase counts via App Store Connect — no per-user data, no identifiers, no contact details.

Third-party SDKs

None. SpellFix does not embed any analytics SDK, advertising SDK, or third-party tracking code. The app links only against Apple's system frameworks and the local AI runtime.

Permissions we request

SpellFix asks for two macOS permissions:

  • Accessibility — required to read your text selection and replace it with the corrected version. This permission is used only locally and never transmitted.
  • Input monitoring — required to detect the double-tap shortcut globally. Keystrokes are not logged or stored; SpellFix only listens for the shortcut you configured.

Data retention

Since we don't collect anything, there's nothing to retain. Settings and downloaded models stay on your Mac until you uninstall the app or remove them manually from Settings → Models.

Children's privacy

SpellFix is not directed to children under 13. Since the app does not collect personal information from anyone, it does not knowingly collect information from children either.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected here with the "Last updated" date at the top. Material changes will also be noted in the app's release notes on the Mac App Store.
